Malo e Lelei! My name is Nanise Lesleyon Vaitohi and I am from Tonga. Tonga is a Polynesian kingdom and is one of the small islands located in the South Pacific with a population of over 100,000 people. Tonga is known as the "Friendly Islands" a name given by Captain Cook when he visited the Pacific Islands. Tonga was also called the "dot" island. This is because of its appearance in the world map as a tiny island with just a dot (.).I am currently a teacher in Liahona High School and started teaching there last year. Teaching was never my goal but I know I have learned so much from this experience. My favorite hobbies are reading, swimming and singing. I have a family and loves to spend time with them in my free time.
